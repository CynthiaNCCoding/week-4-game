Crystal Collector

How to Play

START You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

GAME PLAY There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to the total score.

GOAL You WIN by matching your total score to the random number. You LOSE the game if your score exceeds the random number.

NOTE: The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.

PLAY AGAIN Each time the game is started, the crystal values will reset to a new number!
